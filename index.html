What We Do

Comprehensive Support for Immigrants

Housing Support

Transitional housing for 90 days of stability.

Our Host Home program connects families with compassionate community members who open their homes to provide safe, transitional housing. Participants receive up to 90 days of stability while they work toward independence.

Learn More →

Legal Navigation

Pro se clinics, work permits, and document support.

We guide asylum-seekers through the complex legal process with pro se clinics, work permit applications, and document preparation support. Our volunteers help families understand their rights and navigate the system.

Learn More →

Community Integration

Local service projects for skills and community.

Through our HOPE Community Service program, immigrants and asylum-seekers connect with local organizations to build resume skills, practice English, and become part of the Denver community.
